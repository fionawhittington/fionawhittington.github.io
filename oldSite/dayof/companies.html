Armored Things is using Internet of Thing devices to keep large venues safe from hackers and terrorists.

We believe that we can improve physical and cyber security with integration and control of the devices and systems that are already present in your environment. We help our customers save lives, time and money. Armored Things automates incident response by engaging devices that are already live in many venues, such as lights, cameras, locks, and sensors. We aim to take policy, dusty ‘3-ring binder’ emergency plans, and translate them into instant, technology-based response. Armored Things provides better understanding and control over your environment, which can highlight anomalies and detect unusual behaviors before significant harm is done.
