What is SheHacks Boston?

Our mission is to foster interest in technology for women and close the gender gap in the STEM field!
SheHacks Boston is a 36-hour, student-run hackathon open to all female and non-binary individuals.
Led by women from universities across the country, SheHacks Boston 2019 follows the success of last year's hackathon!
